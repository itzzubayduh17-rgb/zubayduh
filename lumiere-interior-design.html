About the Studio

Where craft meets
contemplation.

"A room should never allow the eye to settle in one place. It should smile at you and create fantasy."

Founded in 2010, Lumière is a design studio that believes great interiors are not designed — they are revealed. We work closely with each client to uncover the emotional essence of a space, then build outward from that core.

Our team of architects, artisans, and material specialists brings a multi-disciplinary perspective to every project, whether a London penthouse or a boutique hotel along the Côte d'Azur.

How We Work

A considered process.

01

Discovery

We begin with deep listening. Through a detailed brief and site visit, we uncover how you live, what you value, and what the space demands.

02

Concept

We translate the brief into a spatial narrative — a mood, a material palette, a set of principles that will shape every subsequent decision.

03

Design

Detailed development: floor plans, elevations, material specifications, bespoke furniture drawings, and full supplier coordination.

04

Delivery

We manage the entire installation, attending site regularly to ensure every detail matches the design intent with precision.
